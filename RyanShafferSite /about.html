Academic background

I am a Marketing student at the University of Colorado Boulder, with a minor in Creative Technology & Design and a certificate in Global Business. My coursework focuses on understanding how brands communicate, how consumers make decisions, and how design influences perception.

Creative interests

Im especially interested in projects that combine creativity with structure. I enjoy visual storytelling, layout and hierarchy, and exploring how small design decisions can change the way information is understood.

I regularly work with tools like Adobe Creative Cloud and Figma, and I enjoy experimenting with new digital and AI assisted creative tools to push ideas further.

What influences my work

Music and digital culture play a big role in how I think creatively. Im particularly inspired by electronic music, live events, and the visual language surrounding them.

Im also interested in travel, culture, and sustainability, and how global perspectives shape branding and business decisions.

How I like to work

I learn best by building and iterating. I prefer to start with structure, test ideas quickly, and refine based on feedback and real world constraints.
